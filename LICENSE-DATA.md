## About the dataset

This is a **limited (half-size) subset** of a larger UAV characteristics
database compiled by the copyright holder. The subset was generated by
stratified random sampling (seed = 42) across Mission × Engine Type ×
Size Class, preserving the category proportions of the full dataset.
The full dataset is not publicly available at this time.

Data points were reviewed from datasheets, official manufacturer
homepages, UAV ARMADA, official data releases, and research papers. The
copyright holder makes no warranty as to the accuracy or completeness of
any individual record.
